humans have weapons

humans can battle in-range as long as they have stamina. out of range, they will only perform one volley. stamina is fully recovered on player phase

default commands:
depending on their personality, HUMANS have one of a few commands:
high-five:
empowered. +10 skill, restore 3 stamina. 1 rng. ends turn.

hug:
happy. +20 hit, restore 3 stamina. 1 rng. ends turn.

shake hands:
professional. +3 power, restore 3 stamina. 1 rng. ends turn.

they stack between eachother, but not upon themselves.
